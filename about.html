Our Story

Kangaroo Play and Learn Centre was founded with a vision to create a joyful, safe, and inspiring environment for young children to begin their educational journey. Since opening our doors, we have welcomed families from all backgrounds and provided a nurturing space where every child is valued and encouraged to thrive.

Over the years, we have grown into a trusted early childhood centre known for our caring staff, innovative curriculum, and strong community spirit. We are proud to see our graduates move on to primary school with confidence, curiosity, and a love of learning.

Our Philosophy

We believe that children learn best through play, exploration, and positive relationships. Our curriculum is designed to foster curiosity, creativity, and confidence, while supporting each child’s unique interests and abilities.

  • Child-centred, play-based approach
  • Inclusive and respectful of all cultures and backgrounds
  • Strong emphasis on social and emotional growth
  • Partnership with families for the best outcomes

Meet Our Team

Our team consists of passionate, qualified educators who are dedicated to early childhood development. All staff are certified in early childhood education and first aid, and participate in ongoing professional development to ensure the highest standards of care and teaching.

  • Experienced, caring teachers
  • Supportive assistants and staff
  • Multicultural and inclusive team
  • Committed to continuous learning and improvement

Why Choose Us?

  • Safe, modern facilities and outdoor play areas
  • Small class sizes for individual attention
  • Engaging, play-based curriculum
  • Strong partnership with families
  • Inclusive and welcoming community
  • Regular updates and open communication with parents

We invite you to visit our centre, meet our team, and discover how we can support your child’s early learning journey.
