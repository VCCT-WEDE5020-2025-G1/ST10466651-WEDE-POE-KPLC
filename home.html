Welcome to Kangaroo Play and Learn Centre

Kangaroo Play and Learn Centre is a vibrant, nurturing pre-school where children aged 2–6 begin their educational journey. Our passionate educators create a safe, playful, and stimulating environment that encourages curiosity, creativity, and confidence.

  • Bright, secure classrooms and outdoor play areas
  • Small class sizes for individual attention
  • Warm, inclusive community for families
  • Qualified, caring staff with early childhood expertise
  • Engaging curriculum focused on holistic development
  • Regular communication and partnership with parents

Our Mission

To provide a joyful, safe, and inspiring environment where every child is valued and encouraged to thrive. We believe in fostering a love of learning through play, exploration, and positive relationships.

Why Choose Us?

  • Experienced and passionate educators dedicated to early childhood development
  • Inclusive approach, celebrating diversity and supporting all abilities
  • Modern facilities with a strong focus on safety and well-being
  • Strong partnership with families and regular updates on your child’s progress
  • Wide range of activities: arts, music, STEM, outdoor adventures, and more

Our Programmes

Our age-specific programmes are designed to support each child's unique learning journey. We focus on social, emotional, physical, and cognitive growth through a balance of structured activities and free play.

Joeys (Ages 2–3)

  • Gentle separation, sensory play
  • Music, movement, story time
  • Outdoor play, early social skills
  • Language development through songs and rhymes
  • Safe, nurturing routines for comfort and security

Explorers (Ages 3–4)

  • Play-based learning, creative arts
  • STEM activities, outdoor adventures
  • Early literacy games
  • Group activities to build cooperation
  • Introduction to numbers, shapes, and colors

Adventurers (Ages 4–5)

  • Project-based learning, early literacy
  • Music, drama, social development
  • STEM discovery
  • Problem-solving and critical thinking
  • Building independence and responsibility

Prep Kangas (Ages 5–6)

  • School readiness, advanced literacy
  • STEM, leadership, independence
  • Collaborative projects
  • Preparation for primary school routines
  • Confidence building and public speaking
